About

As many people across the UK start returning to their everyday activities, here at Swansea University weâ€™ve been thinking about public engagement technologies that help keep people safe from spreading COVID-19. Our invention, Squirty Voter, combines the concept of traditional public voting machines and the now commonplace sanitiser dispensers.

This combination allows people to vote without touching any surfaces and benefit from keeping their hands clean from potential germs. We hope this device helps keep people safer by increasing engagement with hand sanitisers while also providing useful public feedback. The code and list of materials can be found on this website. If you make your own please share it with us using #SquirtyVoter.
